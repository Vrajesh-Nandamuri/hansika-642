About the Department

The George Mason University Computer Science Department is committed to advancing the frontier of computing knowledge through a balanced approach to research, teaching, and service. The department offers a wide range of undergraduate, graduate, and Ph.D. programs. It is a hub of innovation and technology, collaborating with leading industries, government agencies, and research organizations to foster groundbreaking research in artificial intelligence, machine learning, cybersecurity, data science, and software engineering. Faculty members are renowned experts in their respective fields, and students benefit from hands-on learning experiences and cutting-edge facilities.
